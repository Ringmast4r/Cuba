                    3. WHY THIS MATTERS
===============================================================================

[3.1] PUBLIC VS PRIVATE CERTIFICATE AUTHORITIES
----------------------------------------------------------------------
PUBLIC CAs (Let's Encrypt, DigiCert, etc.):
- Trusted by all browsers automatically
- Subject to audit and compliance
- Transparency logs public
- Cannot issue arbitrary certificates
- Revocation is public

PRIVATE/INTERNAL CAs (ETECSA):
- NOT trusted by browsers (warning shown)
- No external oversight
- No transparency requirements
- Can issue certificates for ANY domain
- Revocation controlled internally

[3.2] ETECSA'S POSITION

       = Cuba's ONLY telecommunications provider (state monopoly)

ETECSA controls:
- All internet traffic in/out of Cuba
- All mobile phone service
- All landline infrastructure
- DNS resolution for Cuban users
- And now... their own Certificate Authority
